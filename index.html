FAQ

How long will the Death Star take to produce?
Production timelines are still being drafted, but gathering the raw steel for the project will require over 800,000 years at current steel production rates. We hope to accelerate these rates and bring down the time required to complete the initial phase of the project.
How will the Death Star Project deal with government air and space craft regulation?
The Death Star will be powerful enough to allow the Death Star Project to ignore government regulations. We will be the ones doing the regulating!
Will investors be able to tour the Death Star?
Of course! While tours are not possible before completion due to construction hazards, investors will be offered free tours of the Death Star as soon as it is complete. Test firings of the Superlaser can also be arranged for viewing.
